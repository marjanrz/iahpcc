    Paper Submission

    Authors are invited to submit full-length papers to DCHPC2024 through the online paper submission system by the submission deadline. Submission implies that the paper is original and unpublished (IEEE code of ethics).

    A complete paper must be prepared using the IEEE template (available for MS Word or LaTeX) and follow the blind-submission review format.

    Note: The DCHPC2024 review process is double-blind. Authors do not know the reviewers’ identities, and reviewers do not know the names of the authors.

    Please avoid including any author-identifying information in the acknowledgments or supplemental materials. Also avoid linking to websites that may reveal the authors’ identity.

    Papers must be submitted electronically in PDF format via the submission portal.

    For enquiries, contact: dchpc2024@gmail.com or +989100856079 (SMS or WhatsApp).

About DCHPC Conference Series

  • 2018

  • About DCHPC2018:

    The biennial DCHPC conference series was launched in 2018, with its inaugural event, DCHPC2018, held at the University of Qom, Iran. The conference was co-supported by the DCS Scientific Group, the Informatics Society of Iran, Qom University, Universiti Putra Malaysia, and Auezov University (Kazakhstan).

    Gallery 2018
  • 2022

  • About DCHPC2022:

    The Second International biennial Conference on Distributed Computing and High Performance Computing (DCHPC 2022) is organized jointly by the Informatics Society of Iran (DCS scientific group) and Qom University of Technology. The DCHPC2022 conference is technically supported by IEEE Iran section. Due to the Covid-19, the second series was postponed. This conference is an opportunity for the researchers to share their latest scientific findings in high performance computing, distributed systems and applications.
