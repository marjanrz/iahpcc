  • About DCHPC2022:

    The Second International biennial Conference on Distributed Computing and High Performance Computing (DCHPC 2022) is organized jointly by the Informatics Society of Iran (DCS scientific group) and Qom University of Technology. The DCHPC2022 conference is technically supporeted by IEEE Iran section. This conference is biennial and the first Series of the DCHPC was held in 2018 successfully. The details of DCHPC2018 is available at www.iahpc.ir/2018. Due to the Covid-19, the second series was postponed. This conference is an opportunity for the researchers to share their latest scientific findings in high performance computing, distributed systems and applications. Conference poster is available through this link.

  • 2024

  • About DCHPC2024:

    The DCHPC conference series commenced in 2018, hosting subsequent editions in 2108 and 2022. Regrettably, the dchpc2020 conference had to be canceled due to the impact of the Covid-19 pandemic. The forthcoming third iteration of the DCHPC conference is slated for 2024 in Tehran and will be expertly organized by the Iran Informatics Society's DCS Scientific Group and the esteemed School of Computer Science, Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences (IPM). Esteemed researchers from across the globe are cordially invited to submit their groundbreaking findings on topics germane to the conference's scope and theme.

Paper Submission

The authors are invited to submit full-length papers to DCHPC2024 through the Online Paper Submission Systems, respectively, by the submission deadline. The submission of a paper implies that the paper is original and unpublished (all authors must follow the IEEE code of ethics).

A complete paper should be prepared using the IEEE template (template files for MS Word or LaTeX2e), which is blind-submission review-formatted template.

Note that the DCHPC2024 reviewing is double blind, in that authors do not know the names of the reviewers of their papers, and reviewers do not know the names of the authors. Avoid providing information that may identify the authors in the acknowledgments and in the supplemental material. Avoid providing links to websites that identify the authors.

Papers must be submitted electronically, in PDF format, online(Login)

Enquiries on paper submissions can be addressed to the secretariat at dchpc2024@gmail.com or +989100856079 (SMS or Whatsapp)
